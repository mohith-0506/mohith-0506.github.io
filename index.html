Welcome to Snake Game!!

  • Instructions to the game :

    • Use the arrow keys (up,down,left,right) to control the movement of the snake
    • The blue one indicates snake and the red one indicates apple
    • The snake grows its size by 1 unit when it eats 1 apple

  • Restricted Areas:

    • Top and Bottom rows of the board
    • Left and Right columns of the board
    • In the above mentioned areas, for user convenience, the snake will change its movement automatically in clockwise direction

  • The Game is Over when:

    • The Snake's head touches its body
    • When the snake enters the restricted area, and you try to change the direction of the snake to outside the board

    • After reading all the instructions, proceed to the game by clicking the "Play the Game" button
